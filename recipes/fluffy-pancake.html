Fluffy Pancake Recipe

Fluffy Pancake Recipe Link



Description

This will be your guide on how to bake a fluffy pancake.

Ingredients

  • 30g / 3.3 tablespoon - all-purpose flour
  • 25g / 2 tablespoon - sugar
  • 20mL / 4 teaspoon - milk
  • 1.25mL / 1/4 teaspoon - vanilla extract
  • 2 pieces - egg

Steps

  1. Separate the egg yolks and egg whites from the 2 pieces of egg
  2. Pour 20mL / 4 teaspoon of milk on the egg yolks and mix well
  3. Add 30g / 3.3 tablespoon of all-purpose flour and continue mixing the ingredients
  4. Drop 1.25mL / 1/4 teaspoon of vanilla extract on the batter
  5. Whip the egg whites using an electric whisk, add the 25g / 2 tablespoon of sugar at 3 intervals
  6. Pour 1/3 of the meringue to the batter and mix well
  7. Transfer all of the mixed batter on to the remaining 2/3 of the meringue and mix them thoroughly
  8. Put an even portion of a batter into the pan and cook for 5 minutes in low heat
