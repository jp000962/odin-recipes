Gravy Recipe

Gravy Recipe Link



Description

This will be your guide on how to make your own gravy at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon - butter
  • 1 piece - knorr beef cube
  • 1/3 cup - all purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 cup - water
  • 1/2 teaspoon - pepper powder
  • 2 tablespoon - soy sauce

Steps

  1. Melt 2 tbsp of butter in a pan
  2. Dissolve the 1 piece of knorr beef cube and mix it together with the melted butter
  3. Put 1/3 cup of all purpose flour and mix throughly. We're creating a roux mixture
  4. Add 2 1/2 cup of water and continue stirring the pan
  5. While stirring, sprinkle 1/2 teaspoon of pepper powder and continue mixing the ingredients
  6. Lastly, put 2 tablespoon of soy sauce and continue stirring until it becomes viscous and thick in low heat
