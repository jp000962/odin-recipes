Tonkatsu Sauce Recipe

Tonkatsu Sauce Recipe Link



Description

This will be your guide on how to make a Tonkatsu Sauce.

Ingredients

  • 4 teaspoon - sugar
  • 2 tablespoon - soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup - tomato ketchup
  • 2 tablespoon - worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. Pour 2 tbsp of soy sauce in a mixing bowl
  2. Add 2 teaspoon of sugar
  3. Add 2 tablespoon of worcestershire sauce
  4. Add 1/2 cup of tomato ketchup
  5. Add the remaining 2 teaspoon of sugar
  6. Lastly, mix all ingredients until well-combined
